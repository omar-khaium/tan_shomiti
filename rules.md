# Tan Shomiti (ROSCA) Rules Book

This document defines the rules for running a **Tan Shomiti** (a **ROSCA**: Rotating Savings and Credit Association) in a fair, transparent, and low-risk way.

> Note: This is a practical rules template, not legal advice. If you plan to handle large amounts of money, consider written contracts and local legal guidance.

## 2) Core Principles

1. **Trust + transparency:** Everyone can verify collections, draw, and payouts.
2. **Fixed commitments:** Members must pay on time for the full cycle.
3. **Fairness:** The draw method must be auditable and free of manipulation.
4. **Zero surprises:** No hidden fees, no last-minute rule changes.
5. **Safety:** Reduce default risk before it happens (identity, guarantees, clear enforcement).

---

## 3) Membership Rules

1. **Eligibility:** Members must be known to the group (friend/colleague/community) and agree to all rules in writing.
2. **Identity & contact:** Each member provides full name, phone, address (or workplace), NID/passport (optional but recommended), and emergency contact.
3. **One person, one identity:** No anonymous participation.
4. **Commitment:** Joining means paying the monthly share amount **every month** until the cycle ends, even after winning.
5. **Joining is closed:** No new members/shares after the start date unless the group unanimously agrees (recommended: do not allow).

---

## 4) Roles & Responsibilities

Define roles clearly to avoid confusion and accusations.

1. **Coordinator/Secretary**
   - Maintains member list and calendar
   - Runs meetings and draw
   - Publishes monthly summary
2. **Treasurer**
   - Collects contributions
   - Makes payout after verifying full collection
   - Keeps financial records and receipts
3. **Auditor (or 2 witnesses)**
   - Verifies totals, draw integrity, and payouts
   - Signs off monthly statement

**Rule:** No single person should control everything without oversight. At minimum, use **two-person verification** (Treasurer + Auditor).

---

## 5) Shares & Pot Rules

1. **Fixed shares:** At creation, total shares are fixed. Each share entitles its holder to receive **one pot payout** during the cycle.
2. **No selling shares (recommended):** Shares cannot be sold/transferred after start to prevent fraud and disputes.
3. **If transfers are allowed (optional):**
   - Requires **unanimous written approval** of all members
   - New holder must accept all rules and provide identity/contact
   - Transfer must be recorded with date, reason, and signatures
4. **Pot definition:** The pot is paid out only when the full monthly collection is confirmed.

---

## 6) Monthly Contribution Rules

1. **Due amount:** Each member pays `S × their shares` each month.
2. **Due date & deadline:** Payments must be received by the deadline set in Section 1.
3. **Payment methods:** Allowed methods must be agreed (cash/transfer/mobile wallet). All payments must be recorded with transaction proof or receipt.
4. **Receipts:** Treasurer provides a receipt (screenshot/entry) for every payment.
5. **No partial pot payout:** If the pot is short, payout does not happen unless the shortfall is covered per default rules below.

---

## 7) Draw (Lottery) Rules

1. **Draw schedule:** One draw per month at the agreed meeting time.
2. **Eligibility to win in a month:** A member (or share) can only participate in the draw **after their payment is confirmed** for that month.
3. **One win per share per cycle:** Once a share wins, it is removed from future draws until the next cycle.
4. **Draw integrity:** Use one of the following transparent methods:
   - **Physical slips** (names/shares written identically, folded the same, mixed in a bowl)
   - **Numbered tokens** with a publicly visible mapping list
   - **Simple randomizer** (only if everyone agrees, and results are screen-recorded)
5. **Witnesses:** At least 2 witnesses (or Auditor + one member) must confirm the draw result.
6. **Attendance:** Members should attend. If absent, they may nominate a proxy in writing (message in group chat before draw).
7. **Tie/redo:** If a draw is compromised (e.g., slip falls out, duplicate slip, unclear result), redo immediately with witnesses.

---

## 8) Payout Rules

1. **Payout timing:** Payout occurs immediately after confirming full collection, or within ____ hours/days if using bank/mobile transfers.
2. **Payout amount:** Full pot amount for that month, minus any pre-agreed fees (if any).
3. **Payout proof:** Treasurer posts transaction proof in the group channel.
4. **Winner obligations continue:** Winning does not end the winner’s payment obligation. The winner must keep paying monthly contributions until cycle end.
5. **No forced use:** The winner can use the money for any lawful purpose (unless the group is purpose-specific and agreed otherwise).

---

## 9) Late Payment, Missed Payment, and Default

### 9.1 Late payment (before payout)
1. **Grace period:** ____ hours/days after the deadline.
2. **Late fee (optional):** ____ BDT per day OR ____% (keep it simple and agreed).
3. **Impact on draw:** If payment is not received before draw, that member/share is **not eligible to win** that month.

### 9.2 Missed payment (short pot)
If the pot is short at payout time, choose ONE policy and write it here:

- **Policy A (recommended): Postpone payout**
  - No payout until full collection is complete.
  - Draw may be postponed, or draw happens but payout waits until collection completes.

- **Policy B: Cover from reserve**
  - Maintain a small reserve fund of ____ BDT/month (optional).
  - Reserve can temporarily cover shortfalls; defaulter must repay reserve immediately.

- **Policy C: Emergency cover by guarantor**
  - Each member has a guarantor who must cover missed payment within ____ hours.

### 9.3 Default after winning (highest risk)
If a member wins early and then stops paying, it harms everyone. To prevent this:

1. **Security/guarantee (recommended):** Before the cycle starts, each member provides one:
   - A guarantor (friend/family) OR
   - A refundable security deposit of ____ BDT OR
   - A mutually agreed alternative (documented)
2. **Default definition:** Missing ____ consecutive payments OR ____ total payments without resolution.
3. **Enforcement steps (in order):**
   1) Reminder in group + direct call
   2) Written notice in group chat with deadline
   3) Contact guarantor / apply security deposit
   4) Formal dispute resolution (Section 13)
4. **No exceptions:** Default rules apply to everyone equally, including organizers.

## 10) Multiple Shares per Person (Optional)

If allowed, the group must agree on fairness rules:

1. **Max shares per person:** ____ (recommended: keep low).
2. **Draw eligibility:** Each share is a separate entry in the draw.
3. **Win frequency rule (recommended):** A person cannot win more than once in ____ months (optional) to avoid one person taking multiple early payouts.
4. **Payments:** A member with multiple shares pays proportionally every month.

---

## 11) Fees, Costs, and Compensation (Optional)

1. **Service fee:** If the group charges a fee (for admin costs), define it clearly: ____ BDT/month OR ____ BDT per payout.
2. **Who pays fees:** Everyone equally / Winner pays / Split method (define).
3. **No hidden charges:** Any cost must be announced and approved before the cycle starts.

---

## 12) Records & Transparency

1. **Ledger:** Maintain a monthly ledger with:
   - Member list, shares, due amount, paid status, date/time, transaction reference
   - Pot total, winner, payout proof, remaining balance (should be zero)
2. **Monthly statement:** Posted within 24 hours after meeting.
3. **Access:** Any member can request to review records.
4. **Audit:** Auditor/witness signs off monthly statement.

---

## 13) Disputes & Resolution

1. **First step:** Discuss privately with Coordinator + Treasurer.
2. **Second step:** Discuss in meeting with witnesses and ledger review.
3. **Third step:** Mediation by agreed neutral member(s).
4. **Final step:** If unresolved and amounts are significant, parties may seek formal/legal remedies under local law.

---

## 14) Exit, Replacement, and Removal

1. **No exit without replacement (recommended):** A member cannot leave mid-cycle unless:
   - They find an approved replacement **and**
   - Everyone agrees in writing **and**
   - All payments to date are settled
2. **Removal for misconduct:** Fraud, manipulation, repeated defaults, or abusive behavior can lead to removal by unanimous vote, with settlement rules documented.
3. **Replacement rules (if allowed):** Replacement must accept all obligations and provide identity/guarantee before joining.

---

## 15) Rule Changes

1. **Before start:** Rules can be changed by majority/unanimous vote (choose one).
2. **After start (recommended: unanimous only):** Any change to amounts, schedule, draw method, or enforcement requires **unanimous consent**.
3. **Written record:** All amendments must be written, dated, and shared in the group channel.

---

## 16) Privacy & Conduct

1. **Privacy:** Do not share members’ personal info or financial details outside the group without consent.
2. **Respect:** No harassment, threats, or public shaming.
3. **Safety:** Meetings and cash handling should be done safely; prefer digital transfers for large amounts.

---

## 17) Monthly Checklist (Operational)

1. Confirm attendance/proxies.
2. Confirm all payments received (or apply Section 9 policy).
3. Announce and run draw with witnesses.
4. Announce winner and payout amount.
5. Send payout and share proof.
6. Publish ledger + monthly statement.
